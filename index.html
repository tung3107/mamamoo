Mamamoo (Korean: 마마무), commonly stylized and referred to as MAMAMOO, is a South Korean girl group formed under Rainbow Bridge World, consisting of members Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa. They debuted on June 19, 2014 with the extended play Hello and the single Mr. Ambiguous.

The group is primarily known for their vocal performances and their use of various musical genres in their discography. All of the members have their own solo careers. Their upcoming release is the Japanese compilation album I SAY MAMAMOO : THE BEST -Japan Edition- and the documentary "If I do it, It's HIP".

Members

Solar

Kim Yong-sun (Korean: 김용선), commonly known by her stage name Solar (Korean: 솔라), is the leader of K-Pop girl group MAMAMOO. She is in charge of vocals. Solar started her solo career on April 23, 2020 with the single Spit it out, making her the last MAMAMOO member to debut as a soloist.

Moonbyul

Moon Byul-yi (Korean: 문별이), commonly known by her stage name Moonbyul (Korean: 문별) and stylized as Moon Byul, is a member of K-Pop girl group MAMAMOO, and the leader of the project group Girls Next Door. She is in charge of rap and performance. Moonbyul started her solo career on May 23, 2018 with the single SELFISH, making her the second MAMAMOO member to debut as a soloist.

Moonbyul best performances

Wheein

Jung Whee-in (Korean: 정휘인), commonly known by her stage name Wheein (Korean: 휘인) and stylized as Whee In, is a member of K-Pop girl group MAMAMOO. She is in charge of vocals and performance. Wheein started her solo career on April 17, 2018 with the single EASY, making her the first MAMAMOO member to debut as a soloist.

Wheein best performances

Hwasa

Ahn Hye-jin (Korean: 안혜진), commonly known by her stage name Hwasa (Korean: 화사) and stylized as Hwa Sa, is a member of K-Pop girl group MAMAMOO, and a former member of the project group Refund Sisters. She is in charge of rap and vocals. Hwasa started her solo career on February 13, 2019 with the single TWIT, making her the third MAMAMOO member to debut as a soloist.
